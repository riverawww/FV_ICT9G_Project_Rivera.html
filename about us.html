About Us

A fire prevention plan lists all the potential risks that you identified in your risk assessment and specifies the right safety procedures to adhere to in the case of a fire. A fire prevention strategy can assist prevent a fire from starting in the first place and considerably limit the amount of damage that a fire may do. A fire emergency evacuation plan outlines how you and your personnel can leave the building in a safe and suitable way from any location. This plan also specifies how to contact the fire department and where to store first aid kits and other emergency supplies. This will enable your team to react quickly in an emergency by allowing for forward planning.
